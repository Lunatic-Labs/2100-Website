Gameplay

The Second Wave is a post apocalyptic top down shooter based on the events that transpired during 2020. The game is set in a dystopian universe where robots have taken over the world, and eliminated the majority of mankind in the process. A small team of specially trained individuals take it upon themselves to find a cure and end the robot reign.

Lore

The story starts in Herstelboro, a small town outside of busting Idyll Falls. The group begins their journey by leaving their forest base and venturing out into a nearby town. Harness your character's unique skills to fortify a rebellion against virus altering AI.

Goal

The goal is to find a cure to the Nondescript-Virus-Zombie virus and put an end to the robot domination, as well as find hope in a hopeless world.

Can you survive the Second Wave?
